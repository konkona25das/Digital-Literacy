A Simple checklist for responsible social media use as a college student. 
Do’s
Think before you post and ensure your content is respectful.
Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings.
Share accurate and verified information only.
Use social media for learning, networking, and positive engagement.
Maintain a professional image, especially on public profiles.
❌ Don’ts
Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly.
Don’t spread fake news or unverified content.
Don’t engage in cyberbullying or offensive behavior.
Don’t post inappropriate or harmful content.
Don’t waste excessive time that affects studies or productivity.

This checklist helps promote safe, respectful, and productive use of social media.
