STAY SAFE ONLINE CHECKLIST
General Safety Tips
Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
Never share personal information (address, phone number, passwords) on public platforms.
Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown attachments.
Always verify the authenticity of emails, messages, and job/internship offers.
Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.
Log out from shared or public computers after use.
💰 Financial / UPI Safety Tips
Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank.
Double-check the receiver’s name and UPI ID before making any payment.
Avoid scanning random QR codes, especially those received from unknown sources.
Use only trusted apps like Google Pay or PhonePe for transactions.
🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
Report incidents immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for urgent financial fraud cases

Following these steps can help you stay secure and protect your digital identity.
